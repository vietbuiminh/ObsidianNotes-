# Theorem 2.4
Claim: $\forall P \in \mathbb{L}: 1P = P$
$P + (-P) = \bar{0}$ (by additive inverse of P)
$-P + -(-P) = \bar{0}$ (by additive inverse of -P)
=> P = -(-P) (additive inverse are unique)
P = (-1)((-1)P) 
P = (-1*-1)P (axiom 3)
P = 1P (multiplication in R)
